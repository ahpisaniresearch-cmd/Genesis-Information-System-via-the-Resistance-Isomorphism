## 1. The thesis, in one paragraph (the abstract's core)

A *genesis information system* sits above the resistance isomorphism $\Omega(1/n)=\ln n$ (Paper 1), and that isomorphism may be one of its shadows. The system is **quantity-free** — symbols and relations, no numbers. Most of its computation is **reversible and therefore invisible**: a one-to-one step leaves no permanent trace. The only thing it deposits into our universe is the heat of its **irreversible erasures**, which Landauer prices at $kT\cdot\ln(\text{multiplicity})$. The paper develops and corroborates one reading: **number theory is the erasure-shadow of that system** — what we read as arithmetic is the ledger of where the genesis computation was forced to forget. The individual mathematics throughout is classical; the contribution is the axioms, the erasure framing and its systematic mapping in erasure variables, the reframe that the substrate merely *counts*, and a small number of new formal observations, each graded.

**Voice.** A hypothesis developed into a coherent, internally-consistent system — not a set of proofs. Every substantive claim is graded **Fact** (verified mathematics/computation), **Reading** (a well-grounded interpretation in the framework's vocabulary), or **Lead** (a flagged conjecture or direction). No Reading or Lead is ever dressed as a Fact.

## 2. Framing charter (the load-bearing decisions — apply everywhere)

These are fixed for the whole draft:

- **The erasure axiom is a confirmed *consistent accounting*, not a promoted physical fact.** The end-to-end energy ledger shows energy located only at erasures, equal to the resistance erased, every reversible step free, and both §6 kill conditions untriggered — but *in simulation this is Landauer's principle applied to the bijection-vs-many-to-one distinction, an identity true by construction*. Simulation can confirm the Landauer identity; it cannot *risk* it. Genuine falsifiability lives in the physical-systems direction (the primon gas), which is parked. State this boundary every time the ledger is invoked.
- **No metaphysics in the text.** The genesis system's existence is unfalsifiable by construction (the epistemic barrier is the formal complement of a probe set, Axiom 5), so the paper presents the erasure reading in clean information-theoretic and thermodynamic terms and does *not* argue it via brain-in-a-vat / simulation-of-reality framing. The barrier appears only in its formal (probe-complement) form.
- **The relational result reaches the framework, it does not escape it.** Relay in substance: *$I=\ln\gcd$ reaches the resistance isomorphism's $\Omega_{\gcd}$ term rather than escaping the framework*, and *coprimality emerges-but-isn't-derived*.
- **All three domains stay central at full depth.** Information theory, statistical mechanics (entropy/erasure), and number theory each keep their meaning; nothing is thinned for a target reader. This is a complete capture; audience/journal tailoring is a later, separate operation.
- **The standing negatives.** No leverage on the Riemann Hypothesis; primality-as-output is *relocated, not solved*; "the substrate is plain succession" is the simplest hypothesis consistent with the shadow, not a deduction; no new mathematics is presented as new. Preserve proto/shadow hygiene throughout.
